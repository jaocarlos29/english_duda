Unidade 1: Verb to Be (Presente e Passado)

Explicação:

O verbo "to be" é usado para expressar estados e identidades. No presente, ele pode ser traduzido como "ser" ou "estar", e no passado também pode significar "foi/era" ou "esteve/estava".

Presente:

  • I am
  • You are
  • He/She/It is
  • We are
  • They are

Passado:

  • I was
  • You were
  • He/She/It was
  • We were
  • They were

Exercício 1: Complete as frases com o verbo "to be" no presente.

  • They ___ my best friends.
  • We ___ at the office right now.
  • The weather ___ very hot today.
  • I ___ a student at this school.
  • He ___ a great soccer player.

Leitura e interpretação (Verbo to Be)

Texto:
Mary is a nurse. She works in a hospital and helps sick people every day. Yesterday, she was very busy because many patients needed her help. She is always kind and patient with everyone. Her coworkers say that ela é uma excelente profissional. No último fim de semana, ela estava descansando em casa, mas hoje voltou ao trabalho.

Questões:

1. What is Mary’s job?



2. Where does Mary work?



3. How was Mary yesterday?



Unidade 2: Present Continuous

Explicação:

O Present Continuous é usado para descrever ações que estão acontecendo agora ou em um futuro próximo. Ele é formado pelo verbo to be (am, is, are) seguido do verbo principal com -ing.

Exemplos:

  • I am walking
  • You are eating
  • He/She/It is studying
  • We are playing
  • They are working

Exercício 1: Complete as frases com o verbo no Present Continuous.

  • I ___ (to write) an email to my friend.
  • She ___ (to listen) to music right now.
  • They ___ (to work) on a big project this week.
  • We ___ (to prepare) for a presentation at the moment.
  • He ___ (to read) a very interesting book.

Exercício 2: Transforme as frases em negativas.

  • She is talking to her boss.
  • We are cooking dinner now.
  • They are watching a movie.
  • He is running in the park.
  • I am drinking coffee.

Leitura e interpretação (Present Continuous)

Texto:
Tom is at home right now. He is watching TV while his sister is doing her homework. His parents are in the kitchen, and they are preparing dinner. Outside, it is raining, so the family is staying inside. Tom’s cat is sleeping on the sofa. It is a quiet evening for everyone.

Questões:

1. What is Tom doing right now?



2. What are Tom’s parents doing?



3. What is the weather like outside?



Unidade 3: Simple Past

Explicação:

O Simple Past é usado para descrever ações concluídas no passado. Para verbos regulares, basta adicionar -ed ao final do verbo. Para verbos irregulares, é necessário memorizar a forma específica.

Exemplos:

  • I worked (regular verb)
  • I went (irregular verb)

Exercício 1: Complete as frases com o verbo no Simple Past.

  • I ___ (to visit) my grandmother last week.
  • She ___ (to finish) her homework yesterday.
  • We ___ (to see) a movie last night.
  • They ___ (to go) to the beach last summer.
  • He ___ (to read) a very interesting book.

Leitura e interpretação (Simple Past)

Texto:
Last weekend, my family and I went to the zoo. We saw many different animals, including lions, elephants, and giraffes. My little brother loved the monkeys because they were very funny. After visiting the zoo, we had lunch at a nice restaurant. It was a great day, and we took many pictures.

Questões:

1. Where did the family go last weekend?



2. What animal did the little brother like the most?



3. What did the family do after visiting the zoo?
